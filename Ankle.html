Ankle injury?

Sprained ankle is a common but curable injury. Don't rush things. Remember the RICE method [REST ICE COMPRESS ELEVATE] Make sure you warm up before the stretches and strengthening excersizes. Remember, Temper enthusiasm with patience

Stretchez

Stretching is VERY VERY VERY MUCH IMPORTANT. You should be doing these stretches everyday twice a day or whenever you feel pain. To see the stretches, click on these links ~ Calf stretch Calf stretch II (hold each of these stretches for a minute each)

Strengthing

The following exercizes should be done 3-4 times a week. Resistance bands -Start with your foot pointing down and pull your ankle up as far as you can.Return to the starting position and cycle your ankle 10 times. Balancing pillowStand with your affected leg on a pillow.Hold this position for a count of 10. Repeat 10 times.
