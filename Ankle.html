Ankle injury?

Sprained ankle is a common but curable injury. Don't rush things. Remember the RICE method [REST ICE COMPRESS ELEVATE] Make sure you warm up before the stretches and strengthening excersizes. (hold each of these stretches for a minute each 2 times/day)Strengthening: 3-4 times a week]
